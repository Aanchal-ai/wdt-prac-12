1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products, select your desired items, and click the "Add to Cart" button. When you're ready, click on the cart icon at the top of the page to proceed to checkout. Fill in your shipping details, select a payment method, and complete your order.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and other secure online payment options. Your payment information is securely processed using encryption technologies.

3. Can I change or cancel my order?

We begin processing your order as soon as it is placed, so changes or cancellations cannot be made once the order is confirmed. Please ensure that your order details are correct before completing your purchase.

4. How long will it take to receive my order?

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Shipping times depend on your location and the shipping method you select at checkout. Standard shipping usually takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping can take 2-3 business days.

5. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we do offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping fees and delivery times vary based on the destination. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes that may apply in their country.

6. What should I do if my item is damaged or defective?

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order. We will assist you with a replacement or refund depending on the issue. Be sure to include photos of the damage for a faster resolution.

7. Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes, we offer returns and exchanges on most items within 30 days of purchase. To initiate a return or exchange, please visit our Returns & Exchanges page for more information on the process and conditions.

8. How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track the status of your shipment through our carrier's website or through the tracking section in your account on our website.

9. Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer digital gift cards in various denominations. You can purchase them directly on our website. Gift cards can be used for any products on our site and are delivered via email.

10. How do I contact customer support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, you can contact our customer support team via email at support@trendora.com, or you can call us at +1 234 567 890. Our team is available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM.
