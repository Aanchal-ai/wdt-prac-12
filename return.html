Shipping & Returns

At Trendora, we aim to provide a seamless shopping experience. Below you’ll find information about our shipping and return policies. If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

Shipping Information

We offer reliable and fast shipping options to ensure that your orders arrive safely and on time. Here are the details of our shipping policies:

1. Shipping Methods

  • Standard Shipping: Typically takes 5-7 business days for delivery.
  • Expedited Shipping: Delivers in 2-3 business days.
  • International Shipping: Delivery times vary based on the destination country. International shipping charges apply.

2. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs depend on your location and the shipping method you choose at checkout. We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50 within the United States.

3. Order Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your order is shipped, along with a tracking number for you to monitor the delivery status.

4. Shipping Restrictions

Currently, we ship to the following locations:

  • United States
  • Canada
  • United Kingdom
  • Australia
  • Europe (selected countries)

If you are located outside of these regions, please contact our customer support team for assistance.

Returns & Exchanges

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a simple and easy returns process. Please review the information below to understand our return policies:

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns on most items within 30 days of receipt. To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its original condition, and with all tags attached. Items must also be in the original packaging if possible.

2. How to Return an Item

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer service team at support@trendora.com or call +1 234 567 890 to request a return authorization.
  • Include your order number, the item(s) you wish to return, and the reason for the return in your email or call.
  • Once you receive return instructions, carefully pack the item(s) and ship them back to us. Please use a trackable shipping method to avoid potential issues.

3. Return Shipping Costs

If your return is due to a defect or our error, we will cover the cost of return shipping. For all other returns, you will be responsible for the return shipping fees.

4. Refunds & Exchanges

Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item(s) and process your refund within 7-10 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

If you prefer an exchange, we will process it once the returned item has been received and inspected. You can then place a new order for the item you want in exchange.

5. Non-Returnable Items

Some items are non-returnable, including:

  • Gift cards
  • Items marked as "final sale"
  • Intimate or sanitary items (e.g., lingerie, socks)

Additional Information

We want to make sure you are fully satisfied with your shopping experience at Trendora. If you have any questions about our shipping or returns policies, feel free to contact our customer support team at any time.
